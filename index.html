Bloodborne 2

The Hunt Begins Again

About the Game

Bloodborne 2 is the highly anticipated sequel to the critically acclaimed action RPG. Venture into a new gothic world filled with nightmarish creatures, challenging combat, and deep lore.

Lore Connection: After the events of the first Bloodborne, the city of Yharnam remains scarred by the curse of blood. New dark forces emerge from the depths, and ancient secrets tied to the Great Ones threaten to awaken forgotten horrors. The hunter must face the consequences of past choices and uncover the truth behind the eternal cycle of the hunt.

Features

  • New haunting environments to explore
  • Expanded arsenal of weapons and abilities
  • Intense, fast-paced combat system
  • Rich, mysterious storylines and lore
  • Online multiplayer and co-op modes
